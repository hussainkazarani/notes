## Video Containers
`mp4` &rarr; most compatible <br>
`mkv` &rarr; flexible, multi-track, high quality <br>
`webm` &rarr; web optimized, smaller, modern <br>
`mov` &rarr; apple/editing heavy <br>
`avi` &rarr; old, bulky, legacy

## Image Formats
`jpg/jpeg` &rarr; lossy photos, small, no transparency <br>
`png` &rarr; lossless, transparency, bigger <br>
`webp` &rarr; modern, smaller than jpg/png, supports animation <br>
`gif` &rarr; animated image, huge, outdated <br>
`svg` &rarr; vector, scalable, icons/logos <br>

## Audio Formats
`mp3` &rarr; universal, lossy, small <br>
`wav` &rarr; uncompressed, huge, editing <br>
`flac` &rarr; lossless compressed, smaller than wav <br>
`aac/m4a` &rarr; modern lossy, efficient <br>
`ogg/opus` &rarr; open, very efficient, less universal
